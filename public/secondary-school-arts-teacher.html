SECONDARY SCHOOL ARTS TEACHER (2021 – 2022)

P.A.V.E.L. ASSOCIATION

Responsibilities:

  • Developing age-appropriate activity plans for children with oncological conditions.
  • Creating a relaxing yet stimulating artistic environment conducive to free personal development and expression.
  • Teaching essential skills: attention, imagination, concentration, creativity, observation, spontaneity, intuition, communication and collaboration.
  • Planning and organizing art activities tailored to the unique needs of each child.
  • Providing moral support during difficult periods of treatment.

Results:

  • I provided the children with a positive and dynamic environment where they had the freedom to express themselves artistically and individually.
  • I contributed to children’s holistic development by stimulating their abilities and encouraging them to actively engage in various enjoyable artistic activities.
  • I established relationships based on authenticity and empathy with the children, their parents and the association’s team of social workers.
  • I received positive feedback from care staff, children and parents, stating the beneficial impact of the activities.
